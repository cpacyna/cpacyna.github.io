Chloe Pacyna

Bio

I am a PhD student at Cambridge University on a Marshall Scholarship. With Peter Campbell at the Wellcome Sanger Institute and Tom Mitchell at the Early Cancer Institute, I use genomics tools to better understand development and cancer predisposition in young people. I also like thinking about historical and ethical questions in genomics, teaching future scientists about molecular biology and bioinformatics, riding horses, and painting.

Next year I'll begin as a medical student in the HST track at Harvard Medical School.
